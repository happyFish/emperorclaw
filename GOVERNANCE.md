# Governance

Emperor Claw is maintained by its creator, Jose Zuma (Malecu OU), who acts as
the project's [BDFL](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Benevolent_dictator_for_life):
final decisions on roadmap, releases, licensing, and what gets merged rest
with the maintainer.

Contributions are very welcome under the [CLA](./CLA.md), and the roadmap is
public — see the pinned Roadmap discussion (or `ROADMAP.md`) and open issues.
Disagreement is fine and useful; if you need a different direction than the
project takes, the license lets you fork (under a different name — see
[TRADEMARK.md](./TRADEMARK.md)).

If the project grows to the point where this model is a bottleneck, this
document will change before the bottleneck does.
